Lasagna recipe

Ingridients
    For the Meat Sauce:
  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    • For the Cheese Mixture:
  • 15 oz (425 g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    • For Assembly:
  • 9-12 lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Extra Parmesan cheese for topping

Instrictions

    Step 1: Prepare the Meat Sauce
  1. Cook the Meat: In a large skillet, brown the ground beef or sausage over medium heat. Drain excess fat if necessary.
  2. Add Aromatics: Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook until the onion is soft.
  3. Add Tomatoes: Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 20-30 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken.
    4. Step 2: Prepare the Cheese Mixture
  4. Mix Cheese: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
    5. Step 3: Cook the Noodles
  5. Boil Noodles: If using regular lasagna noodles, cook them according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process.
    6. Step 4: Assemble the Lasagna Layer the Ingredients:
  6. Bottom Layer: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. First Layer of Noodles: Place a layer of noodles over the sauce.
  8. Ricotta Mixture: Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  9. Mozzarella: Sprinkle a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  10. Repeat: Repeat the layers (meat sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella) until all ingredients are used, finishing with meat sauce on top.
  11. Top Layer: Add a final layer of mozzarella cheese and sprinkle with extra Parmesan.
    12. Step 5: Bake
  12. Preheat Oven: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  13. Cover: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (to prevent sticking, you can spray the foil with cooking spray).
  14. Bake: Bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
    15. Step 6: Cool and Serve
  15. Rest: Allow the lasagna to rest for about 15 minutes before slicing. This helps the layers set.
  16. Serve: Cut into squares and serve hot. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
